Page 1 - The Intriguing Opening

"Chabubi plays in the yard with a colorful ball,
He throws it up high - what a joyful call!
But the ball always comes back down to the ground,
'Why is that?' Chabubi asks with wonder all around."

Page 2 - The Great Experiment

"Shoshi the little fox comes to Chabubi's side,
'Let's try throwing other things here!' he cried.
They throw leaves, stones, and toys small,
Everything falls down - how strange and enthralling!"

Page 3 - The Big Question

"Chabubi looks up at the sky so wide,
Sees the sun and moon and clouds that glide.
'Shoshi, why doesn't the sun fall down?
And the big moon, why does it stay around?'"

Page 4 - Starting the Investigation

"'Let's investigate!' Shoshi says with glee,
'I have a cool game idea, you'll see!'
They bring a big magnet and small iron balls,
'Let's see how they're attracted - it'll enthrall!'"

Page 5 - The Magnetic Experiment

"The magnet pulls the balls without a touch!
They jump to it like magic, oh so much!
'Wow!' Chabubi shouts in great surprise,
'How does the magnet pull from afar - what a prize!'"

Page 6 - The Space Connection

"'Now I understand!' Shoshi jumps with joy,
'The sun is like a giant magnet, oh boy!'
'It pulls all the stars and the moon so bright,
Just like our magnet - what a special sight!'"

Page 7 - The Cosmic Dance

"All the stars dance around the shining sun,
Like a giant carousel spinning - so much fun!
They don't fall because they move in circles round,
Dancing in space like happy children bound!"

Page 8 - Back to Earth

"'But what about us?' Chabubi asks with curiosity,
'Why don't we fly to space so free?'
Shoshi explains: 'Because Earth pulls us near,
Just like the sun pulls the moon, my dear!'"

Page 9 - The Familiar Example

"'It's like when mom calls us for a tight embrace,
We run to her because her love pulls us to her space!'
Chabubi smiles: 'The sun is like a big mom so kind,
Hugging all the stars with love that binds!'"

Page 10 - The Practical Activity

"'Let's play a game of the solar system's way!
I'll be the sun,' Shoshi says with pride today.
Chabubi runs around him in circles so fast,
'I'm a planet dancing in orbits that last!'"

Page 11 - The Great Discovery

"'Now I know why everything falls down below!
Earth pulls like a giant magnet, now I know!'
Shoshi claps: 'And why the sun doesn't fall, you see -
Because nothing bigger pulls it down, agree!'"

Page 12 - The Encouraging Ending

"Chabubi and Shoshi watch the sunset so bright,
The sun red and beautiful setting in the night.
'Tomorrow we'll explore more wonders of our world!
